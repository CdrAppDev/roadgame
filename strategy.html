The Solution

RoadGame clusters each team's games into a single block. Same games, same field utilization‚Äîbut everyone finishes by early afternoon.

Traditional Scheduling

RoadGame Scheduling

Games at 8am, 10am, 12pm

70-90 min rest between games

Done by 1:30 PM

7+ hours free, guaranteed

Why TDs Will Switch

Today, TDs have no incentive to optimize for family experience. They get paid per team‚Äîwhether families enjoy the weekend or sit bored at the field.

RoadGame changes the economics. Predictable free time becomes an asset. Families book activities, vendors pay for access, and TDs get a cut of every booking.

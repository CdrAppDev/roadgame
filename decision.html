Step 5

The Proposal

Based on everything we've learned, here's a concrete proposal for what we should build. This isn't a discussion of options—it's a stake in the ground for other founders to react to.

V1 Proposal

A group booking platform for tournament team activities

Help team moms book group activities (restaurants, escape rooms, bowling, attractions) during tournament weekends. Activity providers pay for group leads. Team moms use it for free.

Why We Believe This

Validated: Demand Exists

59% of families try to combine tournaments with activities. They want "tourn-acations." The demand is real.

Validated: They Want Group Experiences

Families don't want individual bookings. They want to do things together as a team. The social experience matters.

Validated: Team Moms Struggle

Coordinating 12+ families for dinner is chaos. Finding restaurants that can seat 25 people on short notice is hard. This is a real pain point.

Likely: Providers Want Group Business

Groups spend more. A team dinner is $500-800. An escape room booking for 20 is $400+. Providers should be willing to pay for these leads. (Needs validation.)

How It Works

1
Team mom opens the app

"We're in Orlando this weekend for a tournament. What can we do with 15 kids and 20 adults?"

2
See group-friendly options nearby

Restaurants, escape rooms, bowling, attractions—filtered for group size and availability.

3
Book with flexibility

Last-minute friendly. "We just found out we're free from 5-8pm. What's available NOW?"

4
Activity provider pays us

Commission on booking, lead fee, or featured placement. Team mom pays nothing.

What Makes This Different From Yelp?

Built for bracket chaos. Tournament schedules change constantly. You don't know if you're free until the last minute. Regular booking platforms assume you know your schedule. We don't. We're built for "what's available RIGHT NOW for a group of 30?"

What We Need to Validate

Before building anything:

?

Would activity providers pay for group leads? Talk to 5-10 restaurants/venues near tournament hotspots. Would they pay $20-50 for a qualified group booking lead?

?

Would team moms use a centralized tool? Talk to 5-10 team moms. How do they currently coordinate? Would they use an app? What would make it worth switching from group text?

?

Can we get tournament-friendly flexibility? Would venues offer flexible cancellation for tournament groups? Last-minute availability? Group holds?
